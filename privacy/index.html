Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the OpenWords app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created by Dzina Maslouskaya (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a free service. The Application does not require account registration and does not transmit personal data to the Service Provider.

Data Collection

The Service Provider does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users of the Application.

Third-Party Services

The Application integrates third-party SDKs that collect anonymized usage and diagnostic data on the Service Provider's behalf:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) — collects anonymized usage data including app events, session information, device model, and OS version. This data is used solely to understand how the app is used and to improve it.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) — collects crash reports including stack traces, device model, OS version, and app version at the time of a crash. This data is used solely to identify and fix bugs.

The Application also relies on Google Play Services (Android) and Apple frameworks (iOS), which may independently collect device information subject to their own privacy terms: Google Privacy Policy and Apple Privacy Policy.

Children

The Application does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13. The third-party services integrated into the app collect only anonymized, non-identifiable usage and diagnostic data.

Security

Since the Service Provider does not collect or store any user data, there is no user data at risk on the Service Provider's end.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact: admin@openwords.ai
